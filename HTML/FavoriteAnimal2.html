Blue-Footed Boobies are called Blue-Footed Boobies because of their two blue feet.

This is a Booby with its new born baby, they lay eggs.

Blue-Footed Boobies have briliant blue feet that they often use in mating rituals, however they are not the smartest birds. Boobies would often land on ships where hungry sailors would easily grab them and eat them.
